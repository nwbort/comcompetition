About the Podcast

Welcome to ComCompetition, the podcast that delves into the reports published by the New Zealand Commerce Commission. Our expert team breaks down complex regulatory information, making it accessible and engaging for listeners interested in competition law, consumer protection, and market dynamics in New Zealand.

Disclaimer: This podcast is not affiliated with or endorsed by the New Zealand Commerce Commission. These podcasts are generated by AI, based on the reports of the Commerce Commission.
